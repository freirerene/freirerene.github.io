So, this January I picked up, once again, one of my hobbies: to watch old stuff. In this case, Ozark (from the olden days of 2017).

Concomitantly, I have been reading up on Asian stuff to, you know, get on the Asian train like everybody else. Specifically I've read [The Story of China](https://www.amazon.com.br/Story-China-portrait-civilisation-English-ebook/dp/B07TGF7V7J/ref=tmm_kin_swatch_0?_encoding=UTF8&qid=1600799201&sr=8-1) and the *Tao te ching*.

Now, I’m probably the fool who makes the tao worthy by laughing at it (well, some portions of it, anyway), but I think I see a relation between these two disparate things I did on my vacation.

My claim, which I’ll leave for the reader to prove — as an exercise — is that there’s a Pink Floyd/Wizard of Oz thing going on between Ozark and Tao te ching.
